cook soba according to package instructions: add soba to boiling water and make sure noodles are separated and fully submerged. check tenderness of noodles after a few minutes beacuse these cook quickly. after cooking, rinse noodles in cold running water and shake to dry. reserve cooking water. to make dipping sauce, simmer 0.5 cups mirin and 0.5 cups soy sauce with 5g kombu and 5g katsuoboshi. kill the heat before reaching a roiling boil and let steep for up to 30 minutes. remove solids from sauce and reserve concentrate for future use. combine equal parts concentrate and cold water to use for dipping sauce. serve soba with tempura, wasabi, scallions, etc.
