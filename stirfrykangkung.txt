slice 0.5 bunch of kangkung into 4 inch pieces. discard the bottom most stems. rinse vegetables and let drain well. heat 3 tbsp of oil in a wok. saute 6 cloves of crushed garlic, 1 tsp of ginger, and 0.5 tsp of shrimp paste in low heat. add vegetable, 1 tbsp sugar, 1 tbsp soy sauce, 0.5 tsp sesame oil, and salt, and cook over high heat for 5 minutes.
