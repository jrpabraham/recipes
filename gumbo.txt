in a large pot, saute 1 lb of andouille sausage until well-browned. do the same with an equal amount of chicken thigh in the same pot. set aside meat for later. finely dice one bell papper, one yellow onion, and two celery stalks gently saute in the same pot. add 1 tsp tomato paste, 1 tsp paprika, 1 tsp cayenne pepper, 2 bay leaves, 1 tbsp thyme, and black pepper to taste. mix well and cook until vegetables are soft and pot bottom is deglazed. in a separate sauce pan, cook over low heat 1/2 cup butter and 1/2 cup flour until it makes a fine brown paste. add roux, sliced sausages, and chicken to vegetable mixture. fill pot with water and simmer for at least 3 hours. salt to taste. serve with rice or bread.
